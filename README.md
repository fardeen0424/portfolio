## Executive Summary & Biography

Fardeen NB is an **AI Scientist**, **Researcher**, and **Tech Entrepreneur** specializing in pre-training large language models from scratch, transformer architectures, and model compression.

His path into artificial intelligence started from a foundation of engineering execution and entrepreneurship. In 2019, at just 18 years old, he co-founded **FX Pattern Pro**, building and launching a desktop-based fintech application to help quantitative traders identify algorithmic pattern structures. This project served as his first major experiment in large-scale data mapping and predictable system logic.

Realizing that the true ceiling of algorithmic logic lay inside advanced neural network frameworks, Fardeen pivoted entirely into foundational deep learning research. He completed his **Master of Engineering in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Cincinnati**, specializing in the core mechanics of transformer architectures.

Today, his work bypasses superficial API integrations to focus on how models process high-dimensional information. He serves as an **AI Scientist at Mercurion AI** and leads the independent engineering initiative **NeuralCrew**. Under this banner, he achieved the complex task of pre-training and post-training a 7-billion parameter language model, **Neutrino-Instruct**, entirely from scratch to champion open-source data curation over raw computing scale.

## Open Source Projects: Neutrino-Instruct-7B

Neutrino-Instruct is a 7-billion parameter language model pretrained from scratch to champion open-source data curation over raw computing scale.

### Specifications:
* **Parameters:** 7 Billion
* **Context Length:** 32,768 tokens
* **Formats:** GGUF / Safetensors
* **License:** Apache-2.0
* **Architecture:** Custom (Neutrino)
* **Language:** English
